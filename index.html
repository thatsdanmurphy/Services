The Offer: A clear, simple website

If you run a business or offer services under your own name, you need a proper home for it. Not a platform someone else controls. Not a template with your photo swapped in. Most sites like this are split across design and development, and things get diluted or never quite land.

I design and build the site myself, end to end. One set of decisions, carried all the way through. Typography, color, layout, and structure are part of the work from the start, built directly in code. The result is something real, not a file waiting to be implemented. More complex systems can be scoped separately.
