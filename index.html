The Offer: A clear, simple website

If you run a business or offer services under your own name, you need a proper home for it. Not a platform someone else controls. Not a template with your photo swapped in. If you already have a site, this is about bringing it back into shape, or starting fresh if it never quite landed. Something that reflects what you do and makes it easy for people to understand and reach you.

Visual direction, typography, color, and layout included. E-commerce and complex systems scoped separately.
